About

I’m Shardul Saptarshi, driven by a mission to design innovative robotic systems that address real-world challenges and advance autonomous technology. Currently pursuing my M.S. in Robotics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, I specialize in autonomous systems, sensor technology, and control algorithms. My hands-on experience includes developing high-frequency sensor systems and custom hardware at New Scale Robotics, as well as contributing to NSF-funded research on digital twin technology, where I enhanced data transmission and 3D printing systems. Proficient in Python, C++, ROS, CAD, and embedded systems, I’m passionate about solving complex engineering challenges and creating meaningful impact through robotics.
